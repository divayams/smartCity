The Green Loop City project is a conceptual smart city design that integrates sustainable practices and innovative technologies. It features zones dedicated to waste management, urban farming, eco-housing, smart mobility, and an Arduino-based EV charging station. Each zone demonstrates how technology and design can work together to create a sustainable urban environment.

The EV Charging Station is the highlight of the project, showcasing a secure and efficient charging solution using RFID technology and Arduino Uno. This innovation promotes the adoption of electric vehicles and contributes to reducing carbon emissions.
